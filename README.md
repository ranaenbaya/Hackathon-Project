# Focus Room

Focus Room Game is a fun pixel-style game where you can track your focus sessions, earn points, and decorate your virtual room. It combines productivity with creativity in a retro, pixel art style.



## Features:

* **Timer System:** Keep track of your focus time with start, stop, and reset buttons.
* **Points System:** Earn points as you focus and use them to buy furniture.
* **Furniture Shop:** Purchase items like bookshelves, rugs, and plants for your room.
* **Drag and Drop:** Move furniture anywhere in the room to design it your way.
* **Automatic Save:** Your points and furniture layout are saved automatically and restored when you reload the page.
* **Grid Snapping:** Furniture aligns neatly on a 20-pixel grid to make arranging your room easier.



## How to Play:

1. Press the **START** button to begin tracking your focus time.
2. Earn points as the timer runs.
3. Open the shop by pressing the **SHOP** button.
4. Buy furniture if you have enough points.
5. Drag and place the furniture in your room.
6. Furniture will snap to a grid so everything lines up nicely.
7. Your points and furniture arrangement are saved automatically.
